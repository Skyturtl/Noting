Principle 1: Cost-benefit Principle 
- Cost-benefit principle: Costs and benefits are the incentives that shape decisions.

Willingness to pay
- Willingness to pay may not always be in dollar or monetary terms. It could be monetary equivalent. Ask yourself: “What is the most I am willing to pay to get this benefit (or avoid that cost)” 
- Don’t confuse “want to pay” with “willing to pay” when doing this conversation
	- want to pay is always 0
