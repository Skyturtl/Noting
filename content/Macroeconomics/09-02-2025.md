Market
- Buyers change demand
- Sellers change supply

### Factors that Affect Individual Demand
- Price of the good (P) — Relationship → Negative (Causes movement)
- Income (I) — Relationship → Positive/Negative
	- Positive relationship → normal good
	- Negative relationship → inferior good
- Price of complexity (P<sub>c</sub>) — relationship → Negative
- Price of substitute (P<sub>se</sub>) — relationship → Positive
- Preference (P<sub>r</sub>) — relationship → Positive
- Network Effects (N) — relationship → Positive
- Congestion (C) — relationship → Negative
- Expectation about future price (E<sub>p</sub>) — Relationship → Positive (All above cause shift)

### Ceteris Paribus
- When we want to see the impact of one factor on demand, we need to keep other factors constant.
